Xinrui Cui

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student at the University of British Columbia, where I am advised by Professor Z. Jane Wang. Before that, I received the M.Eng. and B.S. degrees at Beihang University, and Northeastern University, respectively.

Research

My research interests include computer vision, machine learning, and explainable artificial intelligence. My current research concentrates on explaining what deep neural networks have learned for their outputs. Representative papers are listed below.
